# Website_Brochure-LLM-Model-
LLM-based tool for automatically generating company brochures from website data.
This project demonstrates how large language models (LLMs) can streamline content creation, reduce manual effort, and produce professional outputs in real time. It’s designed for startups, businesses, and creators who want quick, dynamic brochures without relying on traditional design workflows.

 Gaming Industry Angle:-
In the gaming world, presentation is everything — from indie developers pitching their first title to esports organizations seeking sponsors. This project can be a game marketing engine:
- Indie Devs: Auto‑generate press kits and brochures for new games directly from their websites, saving time and boosting visibility.
- Esports Teams & Events: Quickly create sponsor decks or event brochures by pulling content from team or tournament sites.
- UGC Creators (Fortnite Creative, Roblox, UEFN): Generate “game brochures” that explain mechanics, lore, and features of custom maps or mods, making them more discoverable.
